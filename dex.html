About Seira

Graphic Designer & Multimedia Editor

Dexah Maree "Seira" Castillo is a graphic designer and multimedia editor based in Cagayan, Philippines. She has made a name for herself in the competitive field of design thanks to her strong sense of style and love of telling stories through images.

Her impressive collection of adaptable works from her design portfolio demonstrate her ability to work with a variety of styles and concepts. Seira is incredibly creative in a variety of media, from captivating multimedia edits to eye-catching digital graphics. This versatility not only showcases her creative flair but also establishes her as a highly sought-after professional accepting a broad spectrum of commissions.

She uses the following softwares for her work: Adobe Creative Cloud - Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Premiere Pro.
