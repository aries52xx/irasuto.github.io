About NWA

Illustrator and Storyboard Artist

Neil Jonas Limuel Nuñez a.k.a. “NWA” is not just an illustrator and storyboard artist from Isabela, Philippines, but a creative soul immersed in the world of digital art. Specializing in Japanese anime style, his passion for this aesthetic is fueled by a genuine love for all things anime.

While NWA may suffer with the occasional bout of procrastination, hindering his adherence to schedules, his dedication to the craft remains unwavering. A true perfectionist, he approaches each drawing with a meticulous eye, ensuring a commitment to excellence in every stroke.

He uses the following softwares for his work: Clip Studio Paint, Adobe Photoshop, and ibisPaint X.
