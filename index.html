About i-LAST イラスト

Imagination in Limitless Arts and Stories Team

i-LAST stands as the collective identity for a group of exceptionally skilled artists based in the Philippines. Clients can commission these artists with skills in graphic design, multimedia editing, animation, 3D modeling, illustration, and storyboarding. The team is distinguished by its culture of collaboration and understanding of client's goals, which ensures that each project is a tailored masterpiece.

Fun fact: The name i-LAST originated from #イラスト (irasuto), a hashtag within Japanese art communities that means “illustration.”
