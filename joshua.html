About Ouroboros

Animator & 3D Artist

Joshua Madrigo, known by the pen name "Ouroboros," is a highly skilled animator and 3D artist. He is from Tuguegarao City, Cagayan, Philippines.

With a great knowledge of animation principles gained from watching Japanese animation and graduating from Cagayan State University's Multimedia Arts program, Ouroboros expertly animates rich storylines and emotions through his work. As a 3D artist, he excels in modeling, texturing, lighting, and rendering, resulting in immersive environments that transport clients to alternate realities. Whether working in animation, film, or video games, his unwavering commitment to excellence and innovative visual storytelling stands as a testament to his remarkable capabilities.

He uses the following softwares for his work: Adobe Animate, Adobe Photoshop, and Blender.
